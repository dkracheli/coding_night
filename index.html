Wolt delivery recipe

Honey, we are not cooking tonight

wolt delivery guy

Wolt makes it incredibly easy for you to discover and get what you want.
Delivered to you - quickly, reliably and affordably, so save time - keep coding!

Ingredients:

  • Smartphone
  • Credit card
  • At least one available hand
  • Craving for some type of food
  • Basic understanding of operating an app

How to:

  1. Download Wolt app
  2. Sign up as a user
  3. Set up address & payment method
  4. Choose a food category according to set craving
  5. Choose a restaurant from the list of those near you
  6. Go over the menue & question your choice
  7. Choose a dish to your liking
  8. Rethink that choice - if not at peace with it, repeat stages 4-7
  9. Complete order & add comment to restaurant (if needed)
  10. Wait for the order to arrive (prepare the courier's tip in the meantime - recommended)
  11. Open the door with a smile and receive the order
  12. Eat with pleasure & don't forget to rate the restaurant and delivery!
